Multiple Dimensional Lists

We have looked at examples so far where the elements of lists or tuples are

  • strings
  • integers
  • floating-points

The elements of lists or tuples are not limited to the above, they can also be

  • lists
  • tuples
  • dictionaries

You can have lists inside of lists, tuples inside of tuples, tuples inside of lists, and so on. (We will deal with dictionaries within dictionaries in another section.)
